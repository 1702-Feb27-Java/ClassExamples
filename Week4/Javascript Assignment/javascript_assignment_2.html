Fizz Buzz App

Michael Hobbs
2017 March 23

Fizz Buzz is a sequence of numbers where every multiple of 3 is replaced with the word 'Fizz', every multiple of 5 replaced with 'Buzz', and every multiple of both 3 and 5 with 'Fizz-Buzz'. This app lets you define where in the Fizz Buzz sequence it will start and you can also define where in the sequence it will end.
